About Me

Originally from Ohio, I majored in History and Religious Studies at the University of Pittsburgh. After graduating, I started to thru-hike.

Professionally, I worked in marketing research before moving to Reno, NV, where I currently work as an analyst with Patagonia's customer insights and analytics team. In my free time, you can find me exploring the Eastern Sierra on foot or ski.

I also like tea, to-do lists, and indie folk music.
